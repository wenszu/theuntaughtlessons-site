The takeaway

AI makes your structure visible

The prompt shapes the structure. Each prompt angle produces a valid but different MECE output because MECE is more of a discipline rather than a concept that provides one correct answer.

AI follows your logic, not its own. The quality of the buckets reflects the clarity of your instructions. Weak prompt, weak structure.

Your job is not to prompt. Your job is to judge. The executive skill is knowing whether the output is actually MECE, not just whether it looks clean.
