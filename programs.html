How It Works

Three phases. One immersive storyline. Real skills you will use the next day.

Every exercise is built around an immersive real-world storyline. You are not doing abstract drills. You are navigating high-stakes situations where your thinking, communication and presence are tested in the moment.

01

Think Clearly

2 hours 17 minutes

Most people were never taught how to organize their thoughts before they open their mouth. Phase 1 gives you the frameworks to fix that.

Videos

  • Introduction
  • KonMari for the Cluttered Mind
  • Rule of Three
  • Bolded Summary Phrases

Exercises

Seven hands-on exercises including real workplace scenarios where you practice structuring messy information, organizing your thinking under time pressure, and communicating complex ideas with clarity and precision.

AI Integration

In this phase you will use AI as a thinking partner — learning how to stress-test your logic, identify gaps in your reasoning, and compress your ideas before you communicate them.

02

Speak Concisely

2 hours 5 minutes

Clarity in your head means nothing if you cannot land it with others. Phase 2 teaches you how to structure, sequence and deliver your thinking so it moves people.

Videos

  • Wait, What's the Problem Again?
  • Divide and Conquer
  • The Executive Storyline
  • The Art of Persuasion

Exercises

Seven exercises including the Pyramid Principle in practice, building a persuasive narrative from scratch, and high-stakes communication scenarios where precision and confidence determine the outcome.

AI Integration

In this phase you will use AI to sharpen and compress your writing in real time — getting instant feedback on your communication before a high-stakes moment.

03

Act Confidently

1 hour 56 minutes

Executive presence is not a personality trait. It is a set of learnable behaviors. Phase 3 teaches you how to read a room, hold your ground, and lead with gravitas.

Videos

  • How to Read People
  • Let's Switch Hats
  • Speak Like Obama
  • I Have Bad News
  • The Art of Saying No

Exercises

Five exercises including perspective-taking scenarios, body language and silence techniques, and navigating difficult conversations with composure and authority.

AI Integration

In this phase you will use AI to simulate difficult conversations — practicing how to read, respond and adapt in real time before the conversation actually happens.

Total program time: approximately 6 hours and 20 minutes of combined video and exercises.
