Find Your Level

The TSA Score™ measures how clearly you think, how concisely you speak, and how confidently you act — three skills that define how you show up at work and in life.

C³ = Clarity of Thought · Conciseness of Delivery · Confidence in Action

What is the TSA Score™?

🧠

Think Clearly

Can you organize your ideas so others can follow them?

🗣️

Speak Concisely

Can you say what matters most in the time you have?

⚡

Act Confidently

Can you hold your ground in a difficult conversation?

The TSA Score™ gives you a number out of 100 across all three. Take it before training to see where you start. Take it again after to see how far you've come.

How it works

1

Sort your thinking.

You'll organize a set of items into logical groups. No tricks — just clear, structured thinking.

2

Say it out loud.

You'll record yourself giving a short talk, then compress it. The goal is to say more with less.

3

Handle the hard conversation.

You'll play out a realistic difficult conversation with an AI character. Say the hard thing. Hold your ground.

Every exercise is scored automatically using the C³ Rubric™
