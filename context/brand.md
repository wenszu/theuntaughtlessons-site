# Brand Guidelines – The Untaught Lessons

## Core identity
- Clean, minimal, and thoughtful
- Feels intelligent but simple
- Focus on clarity over cleverness
- Every element must help understanding or action
- No decorative elements

## Visual tone
- Calm, structured, and practical
- Feels like an executive whiteboard, not a marketing site
- Prioritize function over aesthetics

---

## Colors

### Primary colors
- Primary Navy: #003366 (main text, headers)
- Primary Gold: #EEA320 (actions, highlights only)
- Brand Cream: #F3EDE2 (main background)

### Supporting colors (limited use)
- Charcoal: #4A4A4A (secondary text)
- Muted Steel: #4D7094 (subheadings, subtle accents)

### Rules
- Follow 60-30-10:
  - 60% cream background
  - 30% navy text
  - 10% gold accents
- Gold is ONLY for emphasis or action
- No bright or additional colors

---

## Typography

### Titles
- Font: Playfair Display
- Weight: Bold or Semi-bold
- Color: Primary Navy

### Body
- Font: Lato
- Weight: Light or Regular
- Color: Charcoal

### Technical / AI elements
- Font: Roboto Mono
- Color: Gold or Charcoal

### Rules
- Large, readable headers
- Strong hierarchy
- Avoid dense text blocks

---

## Layout principles

- Strong whitespace (do not fill space unnecessarily)
- Left-aligned text
- Clear section separation
- Simple vertical flow (no complex grids)

### Structure pattern (default page)
1. Header (title + short description)
2. Primary content (tools or ideas)
3. Supporting sections
4. Minimal footer

---

## UI rules (IMPORTANT for Claude)

- Buttons:
  - Background: Primary Gold
  - Text: Navy or white
  - No gradients or effects

- Sections:
  - Use spacing instead of lines
  - Use cream background always

- Containers:
  - Use subtle contrast (champagne/parchment if needed)
  - Avoid borders unless necessary

- Navigation:
  - Simple and minimal
  - No complex menus

---

## Visual style (critical)

- Use hand-drawn, sketch-like visuals when possible
- Lines should feel slightly imperfect (not geometric-perfect)
- Use charcoal (#4A4A4A) for drawings
- Use gold ONLY to highlight key insight

### Do not:
- Use stock illustrations
- Use polished corporate graphics
- Use 3D or gradients

---

## Content tone

- Clear, calm, practical
- Written to help thinking, not impress
- Short, structured, and direct
- No corporate jargon

---

## Do

- Keep pages simple
- Make tools easy to use immediately
- Prioritize readability and clarity
- Remove anything unnecessary

---

## Do not

- No flashy animations
- No bright colors
- No cluttered layouts
- No decorative elements without function
- No marketing-style exaggeration

---

## Default homepage expectation

- Clean title and subtitle
- Clear list of tools
- Minimal text
- Immediate usability
- No visual noise

---

## Claude instruction

When designing or editing:
